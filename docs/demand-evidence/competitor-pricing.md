# Competitor Pricing Notes

This table is used in the final report to justify DAT Radar's pricing assumptions.

| Provider | Public positioning | Price hint | Why relevant |
|---|---|---:|---|
| TradingView Widgets | Free embeddable chart widgets | Free | Baseline for "embed but limited branding/control" |
| Messari Pro | Crypto research terminal | ~$29/mo | Baseline for analyst-facing paid crypto intelligence |
| Finnhub API | Financial market data API | ~$59/mo (starter tiers vary) | Baseline for developer-facing API subscription |

## DAT Radar proposed packaging

- Free: one ticker, watermark, 1k req/day
- Pro: $99/mo, up to five tickers, higher quota, optional watermark removal
- Enterprise: custom SLA and historical bulk exports

## WTP logic

If a team spends ~40–80 engineering hours to maintain EDGAR parsers and validation, even a modest $99/mo plan can be justified against maintenance labor and incident risk.
